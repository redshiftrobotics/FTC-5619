Pseudo-code for autonomous mode - moving docking pad

Assumes you are starting in the building zone.  Code will change prior to the dashed line for the other side.

Go forward 12 inches.
Spin 90 degrees to the left.
Go forward 12 inches.
Spin 90 degrees to the right.
go forward 48 inches.
----
Spin (90 + 45) degrees to the right.
Go forward 12 inches.
Go forward 6 feet (it's assumed if we push at this angle, it will shove in the corner).
Go backwards 6 inches.
Go right (90 + 45) degrees.
Go forward 3.5 feet.
